Humanity has entered the Dust Age

The world is remnants of a forgotten global holocaust.
In Base Zero, the Chief calls on the four great clans on a journey southwards.

The Fire Fighters

Fira

The Fire Fighters

The Fira are fighters of fire. They dance where the sun licks the earth and wield weapons of flame. Gifted with great strength, when not putting out desert fires for Base Zero, they are hungry to show off their strength and power.

The Thunder Tyrants

Silo

The Thunder Tyrants

The SILO have owned the generators since the birth of Base Zero and thus hold great market power. Natural strategists, they see the invisible forces of Base Zero with clarity. Their wits pitted against, will come as a deadly shock.

The Diesel Divers

Kepa

The Diesel Divers

They call them road sharks. The Kepa owns the dusty roads of Base Zero. They are the blood that keeps Base Zero running, transporting people and goods alike. The road flows with these thrill-seekers, though with their speed, one might only catch a glimpse.

The Radio Runners

Egro

The Radio Runners

The Egro are the signallers of Base Zero, excellent at building necessary connections to other surviving colonies. They are superhuman runners with great agility and charisma, and masters of communication. They also, no one knows why, have a slight glow.
