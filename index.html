Humanity has entered the Dust Age

The world is remnants of a forgotten nuclear holocaust.
In the Citadel, the Imperator calls on the four great clans on a journey southwards.

FIRA

The Fire Fighters

The Fira are fighters of fire. They dance where the sun licks the earth and wield weapons of flame. Gifted with great strength, when not putting out desert fires for the Citadel, they are hungry to show off their strength and power.

KEPA

The Diesel Drivers

They call them road sharks. The Kepa owns the dusty roads of the Citadel. They are the blood that keeps the Citadel running, transporting people and goods alike. The road flows with these thrill-seekers, though with their speed, one might only catch a glimpse.

MELO

The Thunder Tyrants

The Melo have owned the generators since the birth of the Citadel and thus hold great market power. Natural strategists, they see the invisible forces of the Citadel with clarity. Their wits pitted against, will come as a deadly shock.

EGRO

The Radio Runners

The Ergo are signallers for the Citadel, establishing connections to other surviving colonies. They are superhuman runners with great agility and charisma. They also glow slightly green. It might be the water they drink.
